Ad Performance Insights through Statistical Measures

The project compares digital advertising effectiveness between Facebook and AdWords using a year-long dataset of daily performance metrics. Statistical analysis, including hypothesis testing and variance analysis, demonstrates that Facebook ads have higher conversion rates and reliability than AdWords. These insights inform strategic advertising decisions, emphasizing Facebook's superior performance in digital campaigns.

May 24

Enhancing Heart Disease Prediction by Identifying Key Features

This project presents a comparative analysis of various machine learning models for predicting heart disease using a dataset with 14 features. It highlights the efficacy of feature importance evaluation, particularly using Random Forest and CatBoost models, to identify and utilize the top five critical features for model optimization. The refined models achieved notable improvements, with recall ratios and accuracies up to 85%, demonstrating the potential of targeted feature selection in enhancing predictive accuracy for heart disease.

The project outlines a forecasting study designed to optimize bank operations by examining the service usage patterns of basic and moderate need customers at ATMs and tellers. This streamlined approach was shown to significantly improve efficiency in bank systems.
